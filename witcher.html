Witcher HUD Redesign

Unfortunately, I lost all concept sketches except for this one image.

nspired by The Witcher 3, I redesigned the status widget to be more clear about status effects, as well as including the sign.

The original design of the clock was inspired by Don't Starve, but I had a hard time figuring out how to place it in conjunction with the other elements on screen.

I redesigned the clock so that it could lie on top of the minimap and be unobtrusive.

The Witcher has two main fighting styles which have three sub-styles: steel and silver, and fast, strong, and group. I combined these into one element with distinct colors and locations for the styles as I found it easy to confuse them.

One of the difficult aspect of The Witcher for me was remembering keybindings, however not every player will want a reminder of these, so I designed a version without that could be toggled in settings.

All fighting styles disabled. If I were to revisit the project I would turn down the contrast to increase readability.

This is a realistic example of a fighting style that could be used in-game.

I also played with the idea of setting the widget on the side of the screen, but I reasoned that since the player will likely be looking at the center of the screen it would make the most sense to place the widget there as well.
