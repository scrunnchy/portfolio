Pepsi Survival Horror Mockup

The iconic Pepsi wave at the top of the screen with the blood dripping down creates an unmistakable horror atmosphere.

The health bar exists as further Pepsi branding, leaning into the theme of the game.

Pepsi uses lowercase font on their products. I also made sure to include the water droplets that the Pepsi brand would be known to use.

The ubiquity of the Pepsi logo is key to ensuring the game does not turn into "just another horror game" and retains its theming.
